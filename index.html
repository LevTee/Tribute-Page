MACROLEV

DJ & Electronic Dance Music Producer

BIO

At 6 foot 9, MACROLEV is probably the tallest DJ on Earth and certainly one of London’s most exciting.

Regularly breaking into Beatport charts and releasing music on labels like Hot Fuss, Low Ceiling, Stashed Music and Baikonur Recordings, he’s performed at some of the capital’s leading venues, from EGG London to Shoreditch Platform and The Box Soho.

Most recently, his latest single “Boyfriend” featuring incredible singer Oggie and released on Hot Fuss quickly became one of the best selling records on Beatport and reached #21 in Hype Chart. His tracks like “Charlie’s Trip” and “I Was Doing Drugs” received plays on KISS FM UK and huge support from international DJs and tastemakers.

Having graduated from the award-winning Point Blank Music School and the London Sound Academy’s Elite DJ Course, he is mixing and mastering as well as ghost producing for other artists alongside hosting a weekly show on Youtube and Mixcloud called MACROSOUNDS, featuring the hottest dance music from around the globe.
